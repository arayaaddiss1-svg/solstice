## What we collect

When you create a Solstice account, we collect:

- Your email address (for login and account recovery)
- The symptom information you choose to log: hot flash frequency, sleep, mood, brain fog, joint aches, cycle status, and any free-text notes you write

We do not collect location data, contacts, or any information beyond what you directly enter into the app.

## How we use it

Your logged data is used only to:

- Show you your own history, the Arc visualization, and computed pattern insights
- Generate the doctor-visit report you request, for your own use

We do not sell your data. We do not share it with advertisers. We do not use it to train AI models.

## Where it's stored

Your data is stored in a Supabase database ([project region: e.g., US-East / EU]). Supabase's own security and subprocessor practices apply to data at rest; see Supabase's documentation for details. Row-level security is enabled so that only you can read or write your own logs — not other users, and not us, through the app itself.

## Health data note

Symptom logs you enter may be considered sensitive health information under laws in your jurisdiction (for example, HIPAA in the US applies to covered entities like clinics and insurers, not typically to a standalone consumer app like this one — but state-level health privacy laws, like Washington's My Health My Data Act, or GDPR's special-category data rules in the EU/UK, may apply depending on where your users live). **Before charging money or scaling beyond a small test group, get a lawyer's read on which of these apply to you.**
