# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: July 6, 2026**

Solstice ("we," "our," or "us") provides a self-tracking app for perimenopause symptoms ("the Service"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

**Please read this carefully. This Service handles health-related information, which deserves extra care.**

## 1. What We Collect

When you use Solstice, we collect:

- **Account information**: the email address and password you use to sign up (your password is never stored in plain text — it is handled securely by our authentication provider, Supabase).
- **Symptom log data**: information you choose to enter, such as hot flash frequency, sleep hours and quality, mood, and related notes.
- **Basic technical data**: standard server logs (e.g., timestamps, error logs) generated automatically by our hosting provider.

We do **not** collect location data, contacts, or any information beyond what you actively enter into the app.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information only to:

- Provide the core functionality of the app (saving and displaying your logs, generating your personal insights and doctor report)
- Maintain the security and integrity of your account
- Respond to support requests if you contact us

We do **not** sell your data. We do **not** share your health information with advertisers. We do **not** use your data to train AI models.

## 3. Where Your Data Is Stored

Your data is stored using **Supabase**, a third-party database provider, and the app is hosted on **Vercel**. Both providers may process data on servers located outside your country of residence. Each has its own security practices and privacy policies, which you can review at their respective websites.

Your data is protected using **row-level security**, meaning our database is configured so that only you can access your own logs — not other users, and not us in the normal course of operating the app.

## 4. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained for as long as your account remains active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at **arayaaddiss1@gmail.com**. We will delete your data within 30 days of a verified request, except where retention is required by law.

## 5. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights under laws such as the GDPR (EU/UK) or state privacy laws (e.g., CCPA in California), including the right to:

- Access the personal data we hold about you
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data
- Withdraw consent at any time (by deleting your account)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at **arayaaddiss1@gmail.com**.

## 6. Health Data — A Note on Sensitivity

Solstice is a self-management and informational tool. It is **not** a medical device, and the information you log is **not** reviewed by medical professionals as part of the Service. We treat symptom log data as sensitive information and apply the security practices described above, but you should avoid entering identifying information beyond what's necessary (e.g., avoid pasting full medical record numbers or other identifiers into free-text fields).
